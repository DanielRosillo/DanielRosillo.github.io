New to Highbox?, Push a new account!



Highbox works through our specialized Hash algorithms that using a Totem and Hint like entries to makes a unique password.


Register a new Box on Highbox(Push option)for an account(set Totem, Hint, website,user....)


1.- Change your current password to the new that Highbox gives you.


2.- If you want your password for this account, go to Highbox(Pop option) and choose the account then select incantation. (On this part the Hint is showing)


3.- Type your current Totem and press "get".
The password is display on the screen.
